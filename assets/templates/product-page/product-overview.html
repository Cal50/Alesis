The building blocks of
professional music production

The Alesis VI25 lets you take command of your music software with a series of pads, knobs, and buttons. With 25 velocity-sensitive semi-weighted keys with aftertouch and Octa.ve Up/Down buttons, you can expand the keyboard to the full melodic range and play bass lines, chords, and melodies. VI25 also features 8 assignable knobs and 24 assignable buttons for manipulating effect plugins and virtual instruments: open and close filters, adjust volume levels, activate effects, tweak parameters, and more. 16 velocity-sensitive RGB trigger pads allow you to launch clips or finger drum with exceptional response and colorful visual feedback. Plus, pitch and modulation wheels expand the expressive capabilities of your performing.

Plug-and-Go Software

VI25 was designed to seamlessly and effortlessly integrate with your music software. A single USB connection to your Mac or PC provides both power and MIDI functionality, allowing you to plug right into your computer and start making music. With the 5-pin MIDI Out, you can also use VI25 to control external MIDI modules, synthesizers, and more. A LED screen and illuminated knobs, buttons, and pads provide real-time visual feedback and are laid out for an intuitive workflow. Plus, VI25's compact design makes it easy to take this advanced MIDI controller on the road or implement it into any sized studio.

Ableton Live & XPAND!2

Two dynamic pieces of software that enable you to record, produce, and perform with your computer. Ableton Live Lite is a fluid audio/MIDI environment that enables you to spontaneously record, remix, improvise, and edit musical ideas on the fly. Xpand!2 by Air is an advanced virtual instrument that comes with a collection of premium sounds, ranging from acoustic instruments to futuristic synthesizers. Together, these powerful music platforms allow you to create or perform music with VI25 right out of the box.

Features

  • 49 full-sized, square-front, semi-weighted keys with aftertouch
  • 16 velocity-sensitive RGB pads for beat production and clip launching
  • 36 assignable buttons and 12 assignable knobs interface with your music software
  • Pitch and modulation wheels deliver expressive, creative control
  • Seamless, visual feedback via LED screen and illuminated buttons and knobs
  • USB MIDI and 5-pin MIDI Out offer flexible MIDI connectivity
  • USB power and plug-and-play support for Mac and PC
  • Production software included: Ableton Live Lite and Xpand!2 by AIR Music Tech
